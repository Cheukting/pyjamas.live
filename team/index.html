About us

Pyjamas Conf started in 2019, a group of enthusiastic volunteers in Brazil wanted to make Python conferences accessible to everyone at home. In 2020, without the foreseeing of the COVID-19, we wanted to expand the idea of a conference that you can enjoy at your sofa and making it a globally organized highly diverse conference. Our organizers are from different countries around the globe.
